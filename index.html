Know About SYETR

SYETR RAPS is a trailblazing artist reshaping the Indian hip-hop scene. With hits like "I Don't Mind" featuring Saptak 7, the hauntingly soulful "SAZISHEIN" with Amritakshi Datta, and introspective tracks like "Hosh" and "Zehen," SYETR delves deep into themes of resilience, ambition, and life's complexities. His music reflects a unique blend of thought-provoking lyrics and captivating beats, making waves across the globe. Get ready to immerse yourself in the world of SYETR RAPS.
